# Templates

## Purpose

This section contains reusable FinOps templates, worksheets, scorecards, and operational artifacts that organizations can adapt for their own FinOps programs.

The objective is to accelerate FinOps adoption by providing practical assets that reduce implementation effort and promote consistency across teams.

Templates are designed for FinOps practitioners, cloud teams, finance teams, product owners, platform teams, and technology leaders.

# Template Design Principles

All templates should be:

* Practical
* Easy to customize
* Vendor neutral where possible
* Business focused
* Reusable across organizations

---

# Recommended Usage

1. Copy the template.
2. Adapt it to organizational requirements.
3. Establish ownership.
4. Review regularly.
5. Continuously improve.

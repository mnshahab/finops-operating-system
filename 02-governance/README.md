# Governance

## Purpose

Governance provides the policies, standards, controls, and decision-making structures required to manage technology investments responsibly and consistently.

A strong governance model helps organizations balance innovation, speed, risk, accountability, and financial efficiency across cloud, AI, SaaS, data platforms, and emerging technologies.

This section contains governance frameworks, policies, standards, and control models that support a mature FinOps practice.

---

# Governance Objectives

## Financial Accountability

Ensure technology costs have clear ownership and accountability.

---

## Visibility

Provide accurate and timely visibility into spending, consumption, and business value.

---

## Cost Control

Establish guardrails that support sustainable growth and responsible consumption.

---

## Risk Management

Reduce financial, operational, compliance, and technology risks.

---

## Decision Support

Enable leadership teams to make informed investment decisions.

---

# Governance Framework

The governance model consists of four layers.

```text id="0x7l5u"
Strategy
   │
   ▼
Policies
   │
   ▼
Standards
   │
   ▼
Controls
```

---

## Strategy

Defines organizational goals and priorities.

Examples:

* FinOps Charter
* Operating Model
* Strategic Roadmaps

---

## Policies

Define required organizational behaviors.

Examples:

* Cost Management Policy
* Cloud Resource Ownership Policy
* AI Governance Policy
* Chargeback Policy

---

## Standards

Define implementation requirements.

Examples:

* Tagging Standards
* Allocation Standards
* Reporting Standards
* Dashboard Standards

---

## Controls

Provide enforcement and validation.

Examples:

* Budget Controls
* Approval Workflows
* Compliance Reviews
* Automated Guardrails

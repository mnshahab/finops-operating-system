# Purpose

This roadmap defines the phased adoption and maturation of FinOps capabilities across the organization.

The objective is to establish a sustainable cloud financial management practice that improves visibility, accountability, forecasting accuracy, resource efficiency, and business value realization.

The roadmap aligns with FinOps Foundation principles and supports multi-cloud, Kubernetes, and AI workload management.

---

# Scope

This roadmap applies to:

* AWS
* Microsoft Azure
* Google Cloud Platform (GCP)
* Kubernetes Platforms
* AI and Machine Learning Workloads
* Shared Services Platforms
* Product Engineering Teams
* Corporate IT Workloads

---

# Roadmap Principles

The roadmap is guided by the following principles:

* Establish visibility before optimization
* Establish accountability before chargeback
* Prioritize business value over cost reduction alone
* Enable engineering ownership
* Adopt automation where appropriate
* Measure progress using defined KPIs
* Continuously improve FinOps maturity

---

# FinOps Maturity Journey

The organization will mature through three progressive stages.

## Crawl

Primary objective:

Establish visibility and accountability.

Focus areas:

* Cost visibility
* Cost allocation
* Tagging standards
* Budget ownership
* KPI establishment
* Stakeholder engagement
* Initial governance controls

Expected outcomes:

* Clear ownership of cloud spend
* Foundational reporting capabilities
* Baseline financial accountability

---

## Walk

Primary objective:

Improve optimization and forecasting capabilities.

Focus areas:

* Budget management
* Forecasting processes
* Optimization programs
* Commitment management
* Engineering engagement
* Executive reporting
* Anomaly management

Expected outcomes:

* Improved forecast accuracy
* Increased optimization adoption
* Improved resource efficiency

---

## Run

Primary objective:

Maximize business value through advanced FinOps practices.

Focus areas:

* Unit economics
* AI FinOps
* Kubernetes FinOps
* Automation
* Business value measurement
* Product profitability analysis
* Advanced optimization

Expected outcomes:

* Cost-informed decision making
* Business-aligned cloud investments
* Continuous optimization culture

# Phase 1: Foundation

## Strategic Objectives

Establish the FinOps operating model and governance framework.

### Key Activities

* Define FinOps Charter
* Establish FinOps Operating Model
* Define Organizational Structure
* Establish FinOps Governance Framework
* Define Roles and Responsibilities
* Create KPI Framework
* Define Cost Allocation Standards

### Deliverables

* FinOps Charter
* Operating Model
* Governance Framework
* RACI Matrix
* KPI Dictionary
* Cost Allocation Framework

# Dashboard Library

## Purpose

This section contains dashboard frameworks, reporting standards, KPI definitions, and visualization patterns used by FinOps teams to support decision-making across Engineering, Finance, Product, Operations, and Executive Leadership.

The goal is to provide practical dashboard designs that improve visibility, accountability, optimization, forecasting, and business value realization.

Dashboards should drive action, not simply display data.

---

# Dashboard Design Principles

## Actionable

Every dashboard should support a business or operational decision.

---

## Audience Focused

Different stakeholders require different views of the same data.

---

## Consistent

KPI definitions should remain consistent across all dashboards.

---

## Business Aligned

Dashboards should connect technology spend to business outcomes whenever possible.

## Minimize Clutter

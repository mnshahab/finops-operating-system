# Purpose

This document defines the executive-level Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) used to evaluate cloud financial performance, governance effectiveness, accountability, optimization outcomes, and business value realization.

The objective is to provide executive leadership with a concise set of measurable indicators that support strategic decision-making, investment planning, financial governance, and organizational accountability.

These KPIs align with FinOps Foundation principles of visibility, accountability, collaboration, and business value realization.

---

This document applies to:

* AWS

* Kubernetes Platforms
* AI and Machine Learning Workloads
* Shared Services Platforms

* Enterprise Cloud Services

# Executive KPI Design Principles

Executive KPIs should:

* Focus on business outcomes.
* Support strategic decision-making.
* Provide financial visibility.
* Demonstrate accountability.
* Measure optimization effectiveness.
* Align cloud spending with business value.

Executive KPIs should emphasize trends and outcomes rather than operational details.

# Executive KPI Categories

The executive KPI framework is organized into five categories.

## Financial Performance

Measures cloud spending performance against organizational objectives.

---

## Governance and Accountability

Measures ownership, allocation, and policy effectiveness.

---

## Forecasting and Planning

Measures financial predictability and planning effectiveness.

---

## Optimization Performance

Measures organizational efficiency and cost management maturity.

---

## Business Value Realization

Measures alignment between cloud investment and business outcomes.

---

# Financial Performance KPIs

## Total Cloud Spend

Measures total cloud expenditure across all cloud providers and services.

### Purpose

Provide executive visibility into overall cloud investment.

### Reporting Frequency

Monthly

### KPI Owner

Finance and FinOps

---

## Cloud Spend Growth Rate

Measures change in cloud spending over time.

### Purpose

Evaluate spending trends and growth patterns.

### Calculation

```text
(Current Period Spend - Previous Period Spend)
÷ Previous Period Spend
```

### Reporting Frequency

Monthly

### KPI Owner

Finance and FinOps

---

## Cloud Spend as a Percentage of Revenue

Measures cloud spending relative to organizational revenue.

### Purpose

Assess cloud investment relative to business scale.

### Reporting Frequency

Monthly or Quarterly

### KPI Owner

Finance

---

# Governance and Accountability KPIs

## Allocation Coverage

Measures percentage of cloud spend allocated to accountable owners.

### Purpose

Evaluate financial accountability and ownership visibility.

### Calculation

```text
Allocated Spend ÷ Total Spend
```

### Reporting Frequency

Monthly

### KPI Owner

FinOps

---

## Resource Ownership Coverage

Measures percentage of cloud resources assigned to an accountable owner.

### Purpose

Evaluate governance effectiveness.

### Calculation

```text
Owned Resources ÷ Total Resources
```

### Reporting Frequency

Monthly

### KPI Owner

FinOps and Engineering

---

## Tag Compliance Rate

Measures compliance with approved tagging standards.

### Purpose

Support allocation, reporting, governance, and accountability.

### Calculation

```text
Compliant Resources ÷ Total Resources
```

### Reporting Frequency

Monthly

### KPI Owner

Engineering and FinOps

---

# Forecasting and Planning KPIs

## Forecast Accuracy

Measures the accuracy of cloud spending forecasts.

### Purpose

Evaluate planning effectiveness.

### Calculation

```text
1 - (|Actual Spend - Forecast Spend|
÷ Forecast Spend)
```

### Reporting Frequency

Monthly

### KPI Owner

Finance and FinOps

---

## Budget Variance

Measures deviation from approved budgets.

### Purpose

Evaluate financial performance against planning assumptions.

### Calculation

```text
Actual Spend - Budget
```

### Reporting Frequency

Monthly

### KPI Owner

Finance

---

## Forecast Variance

Measures deviation from forecasted spending.

### Purpose

Identify planning and demand management issues.

### Calculation

```text
Actual Spend - Forecast
```

### Reporting Frequency

Monthly

### KPI Owner

Finance and FinOps

---

# Optimization Performance KPIs

## Optimization Opportunity Value

Measures identified cloud optimization opportunities.

### Purpose

Provide visibility into potential efficiency improvements.

### Examples

* Rightsizing opportunities
* Storage optimization opportunities
* Commitment optimization opportunities
* Resource cleanup opportunities

### Reporting Frequency

Monthly

### KPI Owner

FinOps

---

## Optimization Realization Rate

Measures implementation of approved optimization initiatives.

### Purpose

Evaluate execution effectiveness.

### Calculation

```text
Implemented Optimization Actions
÷ Approved Optimization Actions
```

### Reporting Frequency

Monthly

### KPI Owner

Engineering and FinOps

---

## Commitment Utilization

Measures effective utilization of commitment programs.

### Purpose

Evaluate commitment performance and financial efficiency.

### Examples

* Savings Plans
* Reserved Instances
* Committed Use Discounts
* Reservations

### Reporting Frequency

Monthly

### KPI Owner

FinOps

---

## Commitment Coverage

Measures percentage of eligible consumption covered by commitments.

### Purpose

Evaluate commitment strategy effectiveness.

### Reporting Frequency

Monthly

### KPI Owner

FinOps

---

# Business Value Realization KPIs

## Cost per Customer

Measures cloud cost relative to customer base.

### Purpose

Evaluate efficiency at scale.

### Reporting Frequency

Monthly or Quarterly

### KPI Owner

Finance and Product Leadership

---

## Cost per Transaction

Measures cloud cost associated with business transactions.

### Purpose

Evaluate operational efficiency.

### Reporting Frequency

Monthly

### KPI Owner

Product Teams

---

## Cost per User

Measures cloud spending relative to active users.

### Purpose

Support product and business performance analysis.

### Reporting Frequency

Monthly

### KPI Owner

Product Teams

---

## Product Margin Impact

Measures influence of cloud spending on product profitability.

### Purpose

Support strategic investment decisions.

### Reporting Frequency

Quarterly

### KPI Owner

Finance and Product Leadership

# Executive KPI Dashboard Requirements

Executive dashboards should provide visibility into:

* Cloud spending trends
* Budget performance
* Forecast performance
* Optimization outcomes
* Commitment performance
* Governance compliance
* Business value metrics
* Strategic risks and opportunities

Dashboards should emphasize trends, insights, and decision support.

# Executive Review Process

Executive KPI reviews should include:

* Financial performance analysis
* Forecast reviews
* Budget reviews
* Optimization progress
* Commitment performance
* Strategic investment discussions
* Governance effectiveness assessments

Reviews should be integrated into quarterly executive operating cadences.

# Governance Requirements

Executive KPI definitions should:

* Remain consistent across reporting periods.
* Align with organizational objectives.
* Use approved calculation methodologies.
* Be reviewed periodically.
* Support executive decision-making.

Changes should be approved through established governance processes.

* Better strategic decision-making
* Increased accountability
* Improved financial visibility
* Improved forecasting accuracy
* Better optimization outcomes
* Stronger governance maturity
* Improved business alignment
* Greater value realization from cloud investments

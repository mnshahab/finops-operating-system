# AI Unit Economics Framework

## Purpose

This document defines the framework for measuring, analyzing, and governing Artificial Intelligence (AI) costs through unit economics.

The objective is to establish a consistent methodology for understanding the relationship between AI spending and business outcomes, enabling informed investment decisions, improved financial accountability, and optimization of AI resources.

This framework applies to all AI workloads, platforms, models, services, and products across the organization.

---

# Objectives

The AI Unit Economics Framework aims to:

* Connect AI spending to business value.
* Improve visibility into AI cost drivers.
* Support budgeting and forecasting activities.
* Enable cost optimization decisions.
* Improve accountability for AI investments.
* Support executive reporting.
* Establish measurable efficiency metrics.
* Facilitate AI investment prioritization.

---

# What Are AI Unit Economics?

AI unit economics measure the cost of delivering a specific business outcome, service, transaction, or customer value using AI capabilities.

Traditional cloud reporting focuses on:

* Total Spend
* Service Spend
* Infrastructure Spend

AI unit economics focuses on:

* Cost per Outcome
* Cost per User
* Cost per Transaction
* Cost per Feature
* Cost per Business Value Delivered

This provides a business-centric view of AI investments.

# Core AI Unit Economics Metrics

## Cost per Prompt

Measures the average cost of a single prompt.

Formula:

Cost per Prompt = Total AI Spend ÷ Total Prompts

---

## Cost per Conversation

Measures the average cost of an AI conversation.

Formula:

Cost per Conversation = Total AI Spend ÷ Total Conversations

---

## Cost per Inference

Measures the average cost of a model inference.

Formula:

Cost per Inference = Total AI Spend ÷ Total Inference Requests

---

## Cost per User

Measures the average AI cost associated with each user.

Formula:

Cost per User = Total AI Spend ÷ Total Active Users

---

## Cost per Transaction

Measures AI cost relative to business transactions.

Formula:

Cost per Transaction = Total AI Spend ÷ Total Transactions

---

## Cost per Document Processed

Measures document processing efficiency.

Formula:

Cost per Document = Total AI Spend ÷ Total Documents Processed

---

## Cost per Ticket Resolved

Measures customer support economics.

Formula:

Cost per Ticket = Total AI Spend ÷ Tickets Resolved

---

## Cost per Feature Delivered

Measures software engineering value.

Formula:

Cost per Feature = Total AI Spend ÷ Features Delivered

---

# Generative AI Unit Economics

Generative AI requires additional metrics.

---

## Cost per 1,000 Tokens

Formula:

Total Token Spend ÷ Total Tokens × 1,000

Provides normalized comparison across models.

---

## Cost per 1 Million Tokens

Used for strategic planning and vendor comparison.

Formula:

Total Token Spend ÷ Total Tokens × 1,000,000

---

## Cost per Session

Measures the average cost of a user interaction session.

---

## Cost per Generated Asset

Examples:

* Image
* Video
* Audio
* Content Piece

Formula:

Total AI Spend ÷ Generated Assets

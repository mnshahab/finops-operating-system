# AWS FinOps

## Purpose

This section provides practical guidance for implementing FinOps on AWS.

The focus is on improving visibility, allocation, forecasting, optimization, governance, and financial accountability across AWS environments while supporting business objectives and engineering requirements.

This section is designed for FinOps practitioners, cloud engineers, platform teams, architects, finance teams, and technology leaders.

---

# AWS FinOps Objectives

## Visibility

Provide clear visibility into AWS spending, usage, trends, and ownership.

### Focus Areas

* Cost allocation
* Reporting
* Dashboards
* Cost ownership
* Spend analysis

---

## Accountability

Ensure AWS costs are assigned to responsible teams, applications, products, and business units.

### Focus Areas

* Tagging
* Cost allocation
* Showback
* Chargeback

---

## Optimization

Continuously improve resource efficiency while maintaining business requirements.

### Focus Areas

* Rightsizing
* Storage optimization
* Database optimization
* Commitment management
* Kubernetes optimization

---

## Forecasting

Improve predictability and financial planning.

### Focus Areas

* Variance analysis
* Demand planning

## Governance

Establish standards, policies, and controls for sustainable AWS growth.
